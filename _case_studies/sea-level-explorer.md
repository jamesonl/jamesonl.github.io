I am environmentally conscious, and I wanted a compelling visual way to think through what rising water would actually do to coastlines at planetary scale. This lighter version keeps the adaptive hex terrain model, removes the heavier stats layer, and focuses the experience on the globe, the waterline, and the scenario commentary that explains how extreme each step really is.

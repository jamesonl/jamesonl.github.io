I am most useful when a team needs clearer thinking before it needs more software. That can mean forecasting support, decision-system design, AI workflow prototyping, or documentation and tools that make context easier to keep.

## Good reasons to reach out

- You want a forecasting or planning workflow that people will actually use.
- You are exploring AI assistants but need them to stay reviewable and trustworthy.
- Your team has too much information and not enough shared orientation.
- You want a tailored private brief for a role, conversation, or collaboration.

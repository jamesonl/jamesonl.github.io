---
title: Collaboration Guide
date: 2023-11-09
summary: Expectations for anyone pairing with me on research or delivery.
---

- Every project starts with a story, not a scope doc.
- Status updates live in a shared markdown log checked in daily.
- Feedback is written first, then discussed synchronously if needed.

When in doubt, write it down, keep it concise, and let the work speak.
